Learning Skills & Self-Care

Continuous learning and inquisitive mindset are web developer's must-have qualities. Develop your learning skills consciously. Two learning techniques, Pomorodo and Deep Work are presented by Bar Franek in his article. Remember to keep your brain sharp! Take care of your physics, eat nutritious meals and mind the ergonomics as well while studying. Prolonged sitting poses significant risks to the health. Take breaks during the day and do some workout especially for your neck!
